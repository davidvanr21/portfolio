Who I Am

I'm a young digital designer who loves to make perfect user-friendly products.

It doesn't matter if you're on the go, chilling in a hottub, running a maranthon or in a private jet, products need to work as you aspect. My personal goal is to give the perfect experience of products for all people. Everywhere and anytime.

Visual Focused

From a young age I was making websites and films. I've always been busy (and a little bit in love) with the visual side of design. I love making as much as possible sketches and different variations in projects to consider what the best options are.

A little bit nerdy...

I think it's key to have the knowledge of coding to discuss things with developers. With that reason, I'm a little nerdy myself and I love to dive into some coding once in a while. I have much experience with HTML & CSS and also understand the JavaScript language.

Years of experience

I reach my goals of design with having expierence in the following software: Illustrator - After Effects - Adobe XD - Premiere Pro - Photoshop - Indesign - Blender - HTML - CSS - JavaScript
